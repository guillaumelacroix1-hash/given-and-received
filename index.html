Who I am

A space-holder for what is ready to transform.

I am Manuel — a Swiss practitioner based in the South of Sri Lanka. My work brings together Reiki, Life Coaching, energetic cleansing and intuition.

I open a deep healing space where everything that is ready to be transformed, healed or released comes gently to the surface. Often it is an early-childhood experience that shapes the way we process, express or experience life — the work I offer is an opportunity to meet those places with safety and presence.
